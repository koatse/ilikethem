Who is this for?

This online community is for any real estate investors in Ontario who believe in helping out each other is the best way to succeed.
  • Experienced investors share the team behind their success
  • Novice investors replicate the success by starting with a strong team

Why share?

It helps everyone!
  • Help the professionals who helped you - refer them new clients.
  • Help the novice investors - most investors started out not knowing whom to trust.
  • Help yourself - grow your network and reputation.

How is this different?

Other recommendation websites
  • primarily serve the businesses as an advertising platform - businesses ask users to post good reviews
  • you typically don't know the recommender
  • you can't contact the recommender for further inforamtion
ILikeThem.ca is different:
  • driven by user recommendation, not business advertising - users post referrals out of goodwill
  • you have met or will meet the recommender (e.g. at investor networking event)
  • the recommender is happy to provide further information

Who created this?

An amatuer investor who started out not knowing whom to trust and by luck stumble upon great honest people.
